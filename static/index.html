Our Story

We believe that our coffee is a reflection of who we are as a community

Welcome to KOPPEE, a small business started by a group of students in Group 10 class. We believe that coffee is more than just a beverage – it's a way of bringing people together and creating a sense of community. Our passion for coffee started when we realized that many students and teachers at our school were looking for a high-quality, affordable coffee option on campus.

Learn More

Our Vision

At KOPPEE, our vision is to create a world where everyone can enjoy a great cup of coffee without sacrificing sustainability or ethical sourcing practices. We believe that coffee should be more than just a commodity – it should be a way of promoting positive change in the world.

To create a sustainable and ethical coffee industry
We strive to be a hub for coffee lovers
make a positive impact on society by using coffee as a means to promote positive change

At KOPPEE, we understand that sometimes you need your coffee in a hurry. That's why we offer the Fastest Door Delivery – a speedy and reliable delivery service that brings your favorite coffee blends and accessories right to your doorstep.

Fresh Coffee Beans

At KOPPEE, we believe that the key to a great cup of coffee is fresh beans. That's why we're committed to providing our customers with the freshest coffee beans possible, ensuring that every cup is rich, flavorful, and aromatic.
